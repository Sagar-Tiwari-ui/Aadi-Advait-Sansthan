About Aadi Advait Sansthan

Aadi Advait Sansthan is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to holistic community development. Founded in 2005, we work towards creating sustainable change through education, healthcare, women empowerment, and spiritual wellness programs.

Our unique approach combines traditional social welfare activities with yoga and meditation retreats, using the proceeds from our wellness programs to fund our charitable initiatives. This sustainable model allows us to serve underprivileged communities while promoting physical and spiritual well-being.

80G Certified 12A Registered FCRA Approved
